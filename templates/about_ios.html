PreMatch for iOS

The revolution Andover High has long awaited

Need convincing? Read through this page to learn about the app's many useful features!

7+H at Your Fingertips

Check today's schedule, revisit the past, or plan ahead with a comprehensive school year schedule. PreMatch for iOS knows your schedule so you don't have to.

Personalize with Ease

A simple sign in with your k12.andoverma.us Google account is all it takes to integrate your schedule data from PreMatch.org. Move on from confusing letters - learn which teacher you're seeing at any time.

Avoid Morning Forgetfulness

Never bring the wrong materials to school again with customizable daily briefings. Receive notifications displaying your schedule at a customizable time every school day and say goodbye to early morning confusion and mix-ups.

Always Accessible

Available without unlocking your phone, our easy-to-use widget ensures you always know where to go. It even can tell you what block you are in or should be heading to at any time!
