What is PreMatch?

PreMatch is a site designed for students at Andover High School, enabling them to find out which of their friends are in which of their classes before school starts.

How did it begin?

PreMatch is driven off the inspiration that arises from students tending to post their schedules on social media when they get released but before school starts. This phenomenon demonstrated to us that many students are interested in who their classmates will be in advance. Following this need, we have developed a platform for students to share their schedules and receive valuable classmate information in return.
